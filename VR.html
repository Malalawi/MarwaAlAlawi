VR Game Development at GREE Japan

Virtual reality educational game created at GREE Japan as part of my 10 week internship using Unity and HTC VIVE. The game was developed for a joint VR exhibition/ gaming attraction (Arienai Lab) between GREE and Japan’s aerospace company, JAXA. The theme of the exhibition was around the moon, and the purpose of the exhibition was to educate Japanese children about certain aspects of the moon. Aside from game development, I voice acted a game character’s role, Maru, in Japanese..
